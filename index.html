Expense Tracker

Version 1.0.0

A simple, private way to track where your money goes. Designed to stay out of your way — no accounts, no ads, no distractions. Just you and your expenses.

Our Vision

We believe managing your money should be quiet, fast, and deeply personal. Most finance apps want your data, your attention, and your email. We don't.

Expense Tracker is built on a simple idea: the person who knows your spending best should be you — not a company, not an algorithm, not a server somewhere.

Private by default.  Your financial data stays on your device.
Fast to log.  Adding an expense takes seconds, not minutes.
Honest & focused.  No ads, no dark patterns, no upsells on every screen.
Help Us Improve

This app grows based on what real users need. If something is broken, missing, or could be better — we want to hear about it.
